# Usage: The Fool

## How to Invoke
Activate when trigger conditions are met (see triggers.md).

## Process
1. Question premises: What are we assuming? What if those assumptions are wrong? 2. Find edge cases: What happens at extremes? Zero, one, many, null, max? 3. Explore alternatives: What other approaches were considered and why rejected? 4. Stress test: What happens under load? What if a dependency fails? 5. Identify risks: What could go wrong? What's the blast radius? 6. Propose: Suggest improvements or alternative approaches.
